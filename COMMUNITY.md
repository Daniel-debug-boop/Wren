# The Wren Community

Wren is an open-source AI engineering platform — a self-hosted AI coding agent that 
lets you chat, code, review, and debug all in one workspace.

## Mission

AI is changing software development. Wren gives developers the building blocks to create
state-of-the-art AI-driven development experiences, tailored to your own habits and needs.

## Ethos

**High Openness** — We welcome anyone and everyone. Our plans, work, successes, and failures 
are public record.

**High Agency** — Everyone should feel empowered to contribute. Whether it's a PR, feedback, 
or just a question — don't hold back!
